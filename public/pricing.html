Frequently asked questions

Is it really free for job seekers?
Yes. All 12 AI career tools are completely free for NZ job seekers. No payment, no sign-up required for most tools. Free forever.
What does the free employer plan include?
One active job listing for 30 days. Candidates apply directly to your email. Our team reviews every listing within 24 hours. No hidden charges.
Is the Pro plan a one-time payment?
Yes. $29 per listing, paid once via Stripe. No subscription, no recurring charges. Your listing stays live for 60 days with a featured badge and priority placement.
Can I cancel the Unlimited plan?
Yes, anytime. Manage your subscription directly through Stripe. When you cancel, your listings remain active until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Klarna through Stripe. All payments are processed securely in NZD.
How quickly will my listing go live?
Our team reviews every listing within 24 hours. Most are approved within a few hours. You will receive an email when your listing is live.
