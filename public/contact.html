Common Questions

Is FindMeAJob really free?

Yes. All 12 career tools are completely free for job seekers. Employers can post jobs starting with a free plan, or upgrade to Pro ($29) or Unlimited ($99/month) for featured listings.

Is my CV stored?

If you upload without an account, your CV is processed and not stored. With an account, your CV text is saved to enable faster future matches. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your account settings on the job seeker portal and click "Delete Account". Or email us and we will handle it within 20 working days.

I am an employer. How do I post a job?

Visit the Employer Portal, create a free account, and submit your listing. All features are included — unlimited listings, company profile, and logo.

I found a bug or have feedback.

We appreciate it. Email us at hello@findmeajob.co.nz with details and we will look into it.
