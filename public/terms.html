All career tools for job seekers on FindMeAJob.co.nz are provided free of charge. Employer job listings are available under Free, Pro ($29/listing), and Unlimited ($99/month) plans. Payment for Pro and Unlimited plans is processed securely via Stripe. Free plan features will remain free.

4. Employer Pricing, Billing & Refunds

Pro listing — NZ$29 per listing, one-time. A single Pro listing stays live for 60 days with a featured badge and priority placement in AI matches. Once approved by our team (usually within 24 hours), the 60-day clock starts. Pro is a one-off charge per listing — there is no recurring payment.

Unlimited plan — NZ$99 per month. All listings posted while the subscription is active are featured and prioritised. The subscription renews monthly via Stripe. You can cancel at any time from your Stripe customer portal; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Listings posted during an active subscription stay live for their normal 60 days even if the subscription is later cancelled.

GST. Prices are shown in New Zealand dollars. FindMeAJob Ltd will add GST at the applicable rate on invoices once we are GST-registered; you will see the GST line on your Stripe receipt when it applies.

Refunds. Pro listings are fully refundable if you request a refund before the listing is approved and goes live. Most listings are approved within 24 hours, so the practical refund window is the time your listing sits in the review queue. Once a listing is approved and live, the Pro fee is non-refundable, but we will work with you to correct any accuracy issues or extend the listing where a genuine technical problem on our side prevented it from performing. For the Unlimited plan, you can cancel at any time; we do not refund partial months, but no further charges will be taken after cancellation. To request a refund, email hello@findmeajob.co.nz with your Stripe receipt and listing title before the listing goes live.

Failed payments. If a payment fails or our payment processor is temporarily unavailable, your listing will not be submitted. We will surface a clear error on screen and you can retry, or email us to post the listing manually.

9. Job Listing Guidelines

All job listings must:

  • Be for a genuine, currently available role based in New Zealand
  • Include an accurate job title, clear description, and location
  • State the salary or salary range (or "Negotiable")
  • Comply with New Zealand employment law, including the Employment Relations Act 2000 and the Human Rights Act 1993
  • Not require upfront payments, fees, or purchases from applicants
  • Not be for commission-only, multi-level marketing, or pyramid scheme roles
  • Not contain offensive, discriminatory, or misleading content
  • Not duplicate an existing active listing from the same employer
