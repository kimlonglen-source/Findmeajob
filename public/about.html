About FindMeAJob

FindMeAJob.co.nz is a free AI-powered career toolkit built for New Zealand. We help Kiwis find jobs, write better CVs, prepare for interviews, and negotiate salaries — all using artificial intelligence, and all completely free.

Our Mission

Job hunting in New Zealand should not cost money. We believe everyone deserves access to professional career tools regardless of their budget. That is why every tool on FindMeAJob is free — no trials, no credit cards, no catch.

We built FindMeAJob because we saw how much time and stress goes into job searching. Hours scrolling job boards. Agonising over CV wording. Dreading interviews. We wanted to make it faster, easier, and less stressful for every Kiwi.

What We Do

FindMeAJob offers 12 free AI career tools:

Find Jobs — Upload your CV and get matched to real NZ jobs in 60 seconds. Our AI reads your skills and finds the best fit.

CV + Cover Letter Writer — Paste a job ad and upload your CV. AI rewrites your CV and generates a tailored cover letter for that specific role.

Interview Simulator — Practice with an AI interviewer who speaks questions, listens to your answers, and scores you out of 100 with detailed feedback.

Salary Tools — Check what you should earn in NZ, get word-for-word negotiation scripts, and compare job offers side by side.

And more — CV scoring, job ad decoder, career health check, interview question generator, email writer, and a fun career roast tool.
