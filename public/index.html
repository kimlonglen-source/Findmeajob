Questions? Answers.

Is it actually free?▾
Yes. Every tool — matching, CV writing, interview practice, salary checks — is free for jobseekers. No trial. No credit card. No “free tier” that expires. We plan to make money from the employer side, so the jobseeker side stays free.
Do I need to sign up?▾
No. You can upload a CV, get matched, write cover letters, and practice interviews without creating an account. If you want to save progress across devices or get weekly job alerts, an optional email-only sign-in is there.
Do you sell my data?▾
No. We never sell CVs, personal data, or anything else to third parties. CVs are processed to match you to jobs, then you choose whether to save them. See our privacy notice for the full details.
Is it good at New Zealand jobs specifically?▾
It’s built for NZ. We pull live listings from SEEK, Trademe Jobs and Indeed NZ, and the AI is tuned to Kiwi CV formats, NZRN/NZQA conventions, visa talk, and the tone Kiwi hiring managers actually write in.
What AI do you use and how accurate is it?▾
We use modern large language models for the CV and cover-letter tools, plus embedding-based matching for the job match score. Accuracy is highest when your CV is recent and the job ad is detailed. Results are suggestions, not professional advice — always verify independently.
Who built this?▾
A small NZ team. We built findmeajob because the existing job boards feel cold and the big AI tools aren’t tuned to New Zealand. If you want to reach us, use the contact page — a real Kiwi replies.
