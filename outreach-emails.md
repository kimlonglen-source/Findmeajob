## Sending tips

- **Find a real name**, not `info@`. Trawl the About/Team page or LinkedIn. "Kia ora [team]" converts at 1–2%; "Kia ora Sarah" converts at 10–15%.
- **Send Tue–Thu, 9–11am NZT.** Mondays get deleted with the weekend pile, Fridays drift.
- **One ask per email.** No "also could you tweet this / also here's my newsletter".
- **Follow up once, 7–10 days later, same thread.** After that, leave them alone.
- **Track replies in a sheet** (target, date sent, reply, linked y/n).
